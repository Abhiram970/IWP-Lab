The iPhone 13 and 13 Mini received a number of significant upgrades including new cameras, a bigger battery, more storage and a brighter screen.

Some will be disappointed because it's not radically different from previous models,

and the iPhone 13 and 13 Mini are those same reliable friends that seemingly never change.

That's for the base model, which comes with 128GB of storage.
If you buy and activate your phone on a carrier plan, that price drops by $30,
and that's before adding on the impressive deals from carriers.
